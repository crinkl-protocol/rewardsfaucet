FAQ

Questions from the desert

What is rewardsfaucet.fun?

It's the story of the Bitcoin Faucet — and the front door to Crinkl. Verified receipts go in, Bitcoin comes out. Watch Payoff find the faucet, then download the Crinkl app to start earning sats from your own receipts.

Is this real Bitcoin?

Yes — rewards are paid in sats (Bitcoin's smallest unit). They're small and real. Verification and payout minimums apply, and payouts clear after a short hold. No guaranteed amounts, no "number go up" — just proof in, sats out.

Do I have to share my identity?

No. Crinkl verifies that a purchase happened, not who you are. Once a receipt is verified it becomes an identity-free spend token — the signed proof carries no name, no profile, no tracking. Identity stays off the table.

Who is Payoff?

Payoff is a tiny forensic commerce agent who treats every receipt like sacred evidence. In the desert, he chases the receipt to the faucet. At his terminal — thepayoff.ai — he studies what verified commerce reveals.

Is the Crinkl app available yet?

The native iOS and Android apps are coming soon to the App Store and Google Play. You can use Crinkl on the web today at app.crinkl.xyz.
